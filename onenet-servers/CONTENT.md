## BRAND VOICE RULES

1. **Outcome-first.** Lead with what the customer gets, not what the product does.
2. **Short sentences.** One idea per sentence. Break long sentences.
3. **No adverbs.** "Incredibly fast" → "Fast." "Highly reliable" → "99.9% uptime."
4. **No corporate nouns.** Not "leveraging", "solutions", "cutting-edge", "seamless", "robust".
5. **Address the reader as "you" and "your".** The company is "we" or "OneNet Servers".
6. **UK English spelling:** colour, organisation, recognised, centre, optimise.
7. **Naira prices always in amber (#F59E0B).** GBP secondary. USD baseline.
8. **Every CTA is verb + noun.** Not "Learn more" → "See all plans", "Start building", "Get started free".
9. **Zero typos.** Every page proofread before delivery. No exceptions.
10. **Global voice.** Nigeria and UK named explicitly. Global audience implied.
11. **Substantiate every claim.** Not "fast" → "sub-800ms avg load". Not "trusted" → "4.9★ on HostAdvice".
12. **Pricing disclosure.** Always show renewal price in smaller text below the promotional price.

### FAQ — Web Hosting
Q: Can I migrate my existing website for free?
A: Yes. Every plan includes a free migration from your current host. Our team handles the full technical migration — files, databases, DNS, and email — with zero downtime. Raise a support ticket after signing up and we'll coordinate the rest.

Q: What is CloudLinux and why does it matter?
A: CloudLinux isolates each account into its own protected container. If another site on the same server gets a traffic spike or is compromised, it cannot affect your CPU, memory, or disk. Traditional shared hosting does not offer this — one bad neighbour can slow every site on the server.

Q: Is this cPanel or Plesk?
A: All web hosting plans use Plesk — not cPanel. Plesk is the more modern and secure control panel. It offers a cleaner interface, WordPress Toolkit, and built-in security tools. If you're used to cPanel, the transition takes about 20 minutes.

Q: Can I pay in Nigerian naira?
A: Yes. Nigerian customers pay in naira via Paystack — no international card required, no conversion fees. UK and international customers pay in GBP or USD. Pricing displays in your selected currency automatically.

Q: What happens if I exceed my bandwidth or storage?
A: We notify you before you hit any limits. You will not be automatically charged or suspended. You'll have the option to upgrade or purchase additional resources with fair advance notice.

Q: How does the 30-day money-back guarantee work?
A: Not happy within 30 days of your first purchase? Contact support and we'll issue a full refund — no phone calls, no retention team, no questions. Applies to all new web hosting plan purchases. Domain registrations are excluded per ICANN policy.

### FAQ — Domain Transfer
Q: How long does a domain transfer take?
A: Most standard TLDs (.com, .net, .org, .co.uk) complete within 5–7 days. .ng and .com.ng transfers are managed by NiRA and take up to 14 days. We'll keep you updated at every stage.

Q: Will my website go down during the transfer?
A: No. Your DNS records remain active at your current registrar throughout the transfer. Once the transfer completes, there is a brief DNS propagation window of up to 48 hours — but your site stays live the entire time.

Q: What is an EPP/authorisation code?
A: An EPP code (also called an auth code or transfer key) is a password your current registrar issues to authorise a transfer. You'll find it in your registrar's domain management panel. They are required to provide it on request.

Q: Can I transfer a .name.ng domain?
A: No. .name.ng domains are non-transferable. They are issued under the Digital Identity Initiative and can only be managed within the OneNet Servers account they were registered in.

Q: Does the transfer extend my registration?
A: Yes. A successful transfer adds one full year to your current expiry date. If your domain was due to expire in 6 months, after transfer it will expire in 18 months — at no extra cost beyond the standard transfer fee.

Q: What if my transfer fails?
A: The most common reasons are: domain locked at current registrar (unlock it first), incorrect EPP code, or domain registered less than 60 days ago (ICANN rule). We'll tell you exactly what happened and how to fix it.

Q: Can I transfer multiple domains at once?
A: Yes. You can initiate bulk transfers from the client area. For 5+ domains, contact support and we'll coordinate the full process.

### FAQ — SSL
Q: Is SSL already included with my hosting plan?
A: Yes. All OneNet Servers hosting plans include a free Let's Encrypt SSL certificate that auto-renews every 90 days. It's installed automatically when you add a domain. You never need to think about it.

Q: What's the difference between free SSL and paid SSL?
A: Free (DV) SSL encrypts traffic — all most sites need. Paid OV and EV certificates additionally verify your organisation's legal identity, which some payment processors and enterprise clients require.

Q: Will adding SSL break my website?
A: No. Our team handles SSL installation. If your site has mixed content (HTTP images on HTTPS pages), we'll flag it and guide you through fixing it. The transition is invisible to your visitors in most cases.

Q: How does SSL affect my Google ranking?
A: Google has confirmed HTTPS as a ranking signal since 2014. Sites without HTTPS are shown as "Not Secure" in Chrome and Firefox. SSL alone won't take you to page one — but its absence actively hurts your ranking.

Q: Can I get SSL for a domain hosted elsewhere?
A: Yes. Paid SSL certificates can be issued and installed on any server — not just those hosted with us. You'll need server access, or our support team can guide you through the process.
