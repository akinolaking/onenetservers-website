FAQ

Common questions, answered clearly.

Each WordPress instance runs inside its own Docker container. This means one site’s traffic spike, plugin conflict, or security issue cannot affect any other site on the server. It is the same isolation model used by enterprise platforms, available from our Starter plan.
After choosing your plan, you are prompted to describe your site in plain language — business type, colour preferences, and the pages you need. The AI generates a complete WordPress site with appropriate theme, layout, and placeholder content. You review it, then publish or continue editing.
Yes. Every plan includes a one-click staging environment. Push your live site to staging, test updates, theme changes, or plugin upgrades safely, then push back to live when you are ready. No manual file copying needed.
Yes, and it is free on all plans. Our team handles the file transfer, database migration, DNS update, and SSL installation. Most migrations complete within 24 hours and your site stays live on your old host until you confirm everything is working correctly on ours.
All standard WordPress plugins are supported. WooCommerce, Elementor, WPML, Yoast, and WP Rocket work without any restrictions. LiteSpeed Cache is pre-configured on all plans for maximum performance. If a plugin has specific server requirements, contact support and we will advise before you commit.
WP Starter hosts 1 site with 10 GB SSD and 2 GB RAM — ideal for personal sites, blogs, and landing pages. WP Lite also hosts 1 site but gives you 25 GB SSD and the same Docker isolation, making it better suited for growing businesses with more media and traffic. Both include staging and the AI builder.
If you are unhappy within the first 30 days, contact support and we will issue a full refund of your first payment. No partial refunds, no deductions, no awkward conversations. The guarantee applies to all WordPress Hosting plans on their first payment term.
First-term prices are introductory rates. Renewal prices are shown on each plan card as “Renews at [price]”. Annual plans renew at a significantly lower monthly rate than month-to-month. You can always see your renewal price before you commit to any plan.
